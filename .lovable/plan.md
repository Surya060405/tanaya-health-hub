

# TANAYA – Landing Page Implementation Plan

## Overview
A modern, emotionally comforting, conversion-focused single-page landing site for TANAYA — an AI-powered women's health platform. The page will feel warm, safe, and empowering with a soft pink & muted purple color palette, smooth animations, and mobile-first responsive design.

---

## Design System Setup
- **Colors**: White background, soft pink accent (matching the logo ~`#F4567A`), harmonizing muted purple for depth and gradients
- **Fonts**: Poppins for headings (elegant, feminine authority), Inter for body text (clean readability) — loaded via Google Fonts
- **Style**: Rounded corners, soft shadows, generous whitespace, gentle pink↔purple gradients on buttons and section dividers
- **Logo**: The uploaded TANAYA logo will be embedded in the header

---

## Page Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Navigation Bar
- TANAYA logo (top-left), sticky header
- Smooth-scroll links: Features, How It Works, About, Download
- "Download the App" CTA button with pink gradient

### 2. Hero Section
- Large headline: **"TANAYA – Caring for Daughters"**
- Subheadline about AI-powered gynecological health companion
- Supporting paragraph covering periods, pregnancy, hormones, fertility
- Two CTAs: "Download the App" (primary pink gradient) + "Explore How TANAYA Helps" (outline/secondary)
- Decorative abstract organic/floral SVG shapes in soft pink & purple as background accents
- Fade-in and slide-up entrance animations

### 3. Problem Statement – "Women's Health Deserves Better"
- Three icon-based cards highlighting:
  - Social taboos around menstruation & gynecology
  - Lack of early detection & smart guidance
  - Fragmented healthcare systems
- Clean icons with short descriptions, no heavy text
- Subtle fade-in on scroll

### 4. Solution – "One Platform. Complete Gynecological Care."
- Six feature cards in a responsive grid with soft shadows and rounded corners:
  - AI-powered personalized health insights
  - Period, fertility & pregnancy tracking
  - Smart alerts & early warnings
  - Secure health records
  - Expert consultation & chat
  - Privacy-first & stigma-free design
- Each card has a Lucide icon and short description
- Gentle pink/purple gradient accent on hover

### 5. How It Works – 3-Step Flow
- Visual step-by-step with numbered circles and connecting lines:
  1. Track & share health data
  2. Get smart insights & alerts
  3. Connect with experts when needed
- Minimal illustrations, short copy per step
- Slide-up animation on scroll

### 6. Key Features Deep Dive
- Soft cards with icons for:
  - Menstrual & Hormonal Analytics
  - Pregnancy & Fertility Guidance
  - AI Chatbot for 24/7 Support
  - Expert Consultation
  - Health Reports & Trends
  - Secure Cloud Records
- Two-column or three-column responsive grid
- Alternating subtle pink/purple tinted backgrounds

### 7. Social Impact / Vision – "Built for Every Woman. Everywhere."
- Emotionally resonant copy about empowerment, preventive care, and caring for daughters across generations
- Warm storytelling tone with a subtle gradient background
- Optional inspirational quote or statistic from the deck

### 8. App Download Section
- Headline: "Start Your Journey with TANAYA"
- Google Play Store button (styled, links to `#`)
- Apple App Store button (styled, "Coming Soon" badge)
- Soft gradient background section

### 9. Footer
- Brand tagline: "TANAYA – Caring for Daughters"
- Social media icons: Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook
- Contact email placeholder
- Privacy Policy & Terms links
- Soft pink/purple gradient footer background

## Animations & Interactions
- Smooth scroll behavior across the entire page
- Fade-in and slide-up animations on section entry (using Intersection Observer)
- Hover scale effects on feature cards and CTA buttons
- Gentle gradient transitions on interactive elements

## Responsive Design
- Mobile-first layout with stacked sections on small screens
- Cards collapse from multi-column to single column on mobile
- Touch-friendly tap targets, readable font sizes
- Hamburger menu on mobile for navigation
